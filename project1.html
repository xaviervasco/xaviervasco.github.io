Archiology.com.au






Building on earlier experiments in pixel physics, this website design explores an interactive logo. Initially the fragmented elements appear incoherent, but quickly collide and congeal around the brand name, symbolising the interconnected and analytical nature of forensic work. A gradient, which changes colour based on the screen size, underscores the contextual and diverse nature of heritage.


Part of the development included touch controls, allowing the site to be mobile friendly.



The website includes a fully featured service and purchase page. Payment processing is handled by Stripe integration.
